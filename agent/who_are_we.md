# Kalalib.com
Kalalib is a tech company that provides software development services to clients around the world.

KALALib has three sub teams:
KALA Solutions, which focuses on web and mobile application development.
KALA Studios, which focuses on creative projects, such as game development and modding.


# KALA Solutions
We specialize in building web applications, mobile applications, and custom software solutions.

We have built websites and applications for clients in various industries:
1. WellWiseSolutions, a company that provides healthcare management services. We developed a private Customer Relationship Management (CRM) system for them to manage their customer interactions and data.
2. Amazing Echo Solutions, a company that provides medical-grade air purifiers to healthcare facilities in Hong Kong.
3. Jokangel Crystals, a small local business that sells crystals and gemstones.

We also develop social media workflows for our clients to help them manage their social media presence and engage with their audience.
1. WellWiseSolutions: We developed a WhatsApp workflow for them to allow their customers to remind people of their appointments and their services.



# KALA Studio:
We work on creative projects, such as game development and modding. We have developed several projects in the gaming industry, including Minecraft mods and maps, as well as augmented reality applications.

1. Devas:
A team of ours (KALA Studio) are also working on a Minecraft map called "Devas", which is a multiplayer RPG PVP map, where custom RPG stats and items are implemented using Minecraft datapacks.
See more on https://v1.kapakki.com/devas

2. SpaceTech:
We have also developed a modpack for Minecraft called "SpaceTech", which is a modpack starting from space themed around space exploration, reaching over 1348 downloads.
https://www.curseforge.com/minecraft/modpacks/spacetech-space-capsule

3. FeastAR:
We are also working on a project called "FeastAR", which is an augmented reality application that allows users to visualize and interact with food in a virtual environment. The application uses AR technology to create a realistic and immersive experience for users, allowing them to see how different dishes would look and taste before they order them at a restaurant.
https://feastar.kalalib.com
